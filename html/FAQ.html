Frequently Asked Questions

The Creative Cafe on the Web

  1. What is the purpose of this site?

    2. This site, "The Creative Cafe on the Web", is meant to be a site where you can find inspiration and just browse around for fun. This site was intended for writers who have a writer's block or anyone who is bored and needs ideas. I have provided writing prompts and photos and various other content on this site for stimulating your imagination. Generally, I will update it once a month. I hope it's of use to you!

  2. Can I use content for free from this site?

    3. Yes, only as long as it's for personal use and you cite the source properly. You cannot distribute content from this site for your own profit.

  3. Is it possible for me to leave comments or give feedback?

    4. Unfortunately, this site does not have a comment section yet. Until I create one, you can email me at angelaxz123@icloud.com.

  4. What if I have something to add to this site?

    5. If you have more material that could be inspiration for writers, you are by all means welcome to share it with me! Email me at angelaxz123@icloud.com and I will put it into my site if I think it's appropriate. :)
